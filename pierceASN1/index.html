About Anna Pierce

My name is Anna Pierce, and I am an introvert who finds joy in going on adventures in nature, reading books, creating art, and having coffee with friends.

I grew up in a big family. Being one of the youngest out of seven siblings, I have a lot of older siblings to look up to, and two younger brothers to be a big sister for. My siblings are my best friends. Now, my brothers and sisters have moved to different states and countries. This has ignited my desire to travel the world and visit my siblings. And my dream is to make this possible by becoming a freelance Web Designer. A job that would allow me the freedom to travel the world and work from anywhere is one of my priorities! I'm thankful for my opportunity at Wake Tech to make this dream a reality.

I am a beginner in Web Design and have much to learn. I am eager to learn and grow this semester as I develop new skills and work hard. Last semester I took a Web Development Tools class and learned a lot from it. Even though my work is far from perfect, I enjoy reviewing my final project, which I spent many hours perfecting. This is my website of a Biking Company named Vicious Cycles.
